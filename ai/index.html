Smart Parking AI Project

This project showcases an innovative smart parking lot prototype application developed using AI tools, including Lovable. The application operates by recognizing vehicle license plates, reserving a dedicated parking space for the user, and efficiently navigating them to the most suitable spot within the parking facility.

The parking space is automatically tailored to the vehicle's needs: electric cars are directed to charging stations, larger vehicles like trucks are guided to extended parking spots, and so on. This solution optimizes the parking experience and reduces congestion in a smart and efficient manner.
